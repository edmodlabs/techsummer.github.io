1.3) Experiments in WebVR

Overview

In this section you will explore WebVR content from around the web to find something cool to share with others.

Explore the World of WebVR

Explore Experiences
Try a bunch of WebVR experiences. Good places to look include the Resources and the following:
  • https://patches.vizor.io/
  • https://www.webvrexperiments.com/
    • - We love Mr. Nom Nom and Konterball. Can you beat our high score of 5?
  • https://www.chromeexperiments.com/virtual-reality
  • https://www.reddit.com/r/WebVR/
  • Search for “WebVR” in any browser to find experiences on your own!

Share WebVR

Social Activity
Find a cool WebVR experience and post to the Facebook group. Include the Title of the WebVR experience, a link to the experience, and at least one reason you like it!
